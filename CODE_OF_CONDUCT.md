# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive.
- Giving and receiving feedback professionally.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or bad-faith engagement.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting
Report incidents to: `sanara.lab@gmail.com`

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
